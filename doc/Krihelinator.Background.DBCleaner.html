    krihelinator v0.0.1 Krihelinator.Background.DBCleaner

    Every :db_cleaner_period milliseconds do the following:

    1. Sorted by krihelimeter, keep only the first :max_repos_to_keep rows.
    2. Update the krihelimeter threshold of the DataHandler to the new minimum value.
